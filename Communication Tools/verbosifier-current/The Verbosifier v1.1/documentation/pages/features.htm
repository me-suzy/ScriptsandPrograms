New in v1.1

v1.1 has one new feature: automatic link creation. Whenever a URL is added it is automatically converted into a link. This, for reasons too nerdy to elaborate on, improves the protection you get from Spamguard Extra.

What's so good about The Verbosifier?

  • Thorough customisability. Choose the number of shouts to display; the format of the date; the look of each shout, the input form and the whole shoutbox page; even decide if you want to keep old shouts or if The Verbosifier should delete extras.
  • Tough anti-spam measures. Spamguard Extra — the same anti-spam technology in the Writer's Block CMS — scans the given URL and the actual text of all shouts for spam-like links and stops the spambot dead in its tracks.
  • Friendly to all. The easy templating system makes it simple for you to design the look and feel of your shoutbox, while conveniences like automatic remembrance of shouters and URL-to-link conversion make it easy for end-users to shout their opinions in style.
